About Me

A young developer who tinkers, breaks, and rebuilds with curiosity.

I'm Levy — known online as vLoon. I'm in my eleventh year at SMANDA (SMA Negeri 2 Amlapura) in Bali, Indonesia. I've been obsessed with how software works from the inside out: modding games, scripting automation, building tools, and occasionally writing things that look slightly dangerous (for educational purposes, of course).

My mantra is simple — if I don't know how something works, I take it apart. If it breaks, I learn. Then I build something better.
